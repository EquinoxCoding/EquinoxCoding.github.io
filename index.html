About

WinterHacks is a hackathon run by high school students aiming to bring innovation to every season.

This winter, we challenge you to ignite your creativity. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned hacker, you will find the opportunity to let your passions show at WinterHacks.

FAQ

Q: What is a hackathon?
A: A hackathon is a tech-related competition where students (typically in groups of 4) collaborate to create and submit a project under a time limit. At the end, projects are presented and judged. There are different themes to compete under with the winner of each receiving a prize.
Q: When will the hackathon happen?
A: WinterHacks will take place between Jan 12-13, 2024. It will begin and end in the evening.
Q: What if I have never attended a hackathon before?
A: Don't worry! Hackers of all skills levels are expected and welcome.
Q: How can I join?
A: You can sign up with the registration form right now!
Q: Am I eligible to join?
A: All high school students are able to attend. All experience levels are welcome
Q: What do I need to participate?
A: The only things necessary to participate are an electronic device and a stable internet connection.
Q: Where can I find updates?
A: You can find updates on our Instagram, @winterh4cks. You can contact us at winterhacks@equinoxcoding.ca if you have any questions.
